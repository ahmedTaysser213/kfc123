About Us

Welcome to KFC. This website was created as a central hub for our community where users can find important information, updates, and guidelines in one simple place. Our goal is to keep everything organized, clear, and easy to access for everyone.

We believe in building a strong and active community where members can stay connected, informed, and involved. Whether you are new or already part of the community, this site is designed to help you understand everything you need quickly without confusion.

Through the navigation menu, you can explore different sections such as rules, joining information, and our Discord server. Each section is made to keep you updated and guide you through everything step by step.

Our team is constantly working on improving the experience, adding new features, and making sure the community stays active and enjoyable for everyone.

Thank you for being part of KFC. We’re glad to have you here.

Rules

To keep the community clean and enjoyable for everyone, please follow these rules:

  • Be respectful to all members. No harassment or hate speech.
  • No spamming in chat or channels.
  • Do not share illegal or harmful content.
  • Follow staff instructions at all times.
  • No advertising without permission.

Breaking these rules may result in warnings, kicks, or bans depending on severity.
